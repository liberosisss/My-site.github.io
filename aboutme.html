Liberosis

I don't really like to talk about myself, so I don't want to divulge some of my personal information (such as name, age, my nationality, etc.) I think I appreciate it more when people are interested in my projects than in my personal life. However, since we're on this page, I suppose I should share some information about myself.
So, in my free time, I like to draw and daydream. I have many different hobbies, for example, wood carving, scrapbooking and doing jigsaw puzzles. I also enjoy basic things like listening to music and playing computer games.
My favorite music bands are Reel Big Fish, Sounds of Salvation, Deluxtone Rockets, The Aquabats and The Pietasters. As you probably already guessed, I love ska music.
My favorite games are Subnautica, The Binding of Isaac, Balatro, Portal 2, Picayune Dreams and Pixel Dungeon. I recently bought Mewgenics and I hope it will be good. At the moment, I'm learning C# to make my own games. They will all be free and available on this website or on itch.io
Besides all of the above, I'm also interested in indie animation.
